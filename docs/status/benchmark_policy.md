# Benchmark policy

## Primary benchmark for HG002
Use GIAB/NIST HG002 v5.0q as the primary benchmark where matching truth VCF and benchmark BED are available.

## Secondary benchmark
Keep v4.2.1 as a secondary comparability benchmark for:
- older literature/tool comparisons
- chromosomes/intervals where local v5.0q benchmark assets are not yet staged

## Rule
Do not mix truth VCF and BED across benchmark versions.
Use only matched benchmark bundles per chromosome/interval.
